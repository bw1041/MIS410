Our Little History

Durham House of Pets was founded by Maria Cherries in 2003. As soon as Cherries graduated from the University of New Hampshire with a degree in Animal Care, she decided to turn her savings into a business providing quality goods for the pets we love. Attending the university, she noticed the number students living with their pets on campus have limited access to animal products. The number of service animals, Health and Wellness Program from UNH brings in during testing season are limited to pet supplies such as dog food. Students and staff members will have to drive miles in order to purchase these supplies. Some students do not have the necessities to do so, thus Cherries bought pet supplies to the residents of Durham, NH. Now, our headquarter is in New York, New York with another regional branch in Manchester, NH!

Here at Durham House of Pets, we offer a variety of products that are fun, secure, user-friendly, and safe. Our products are always developing and the store has expanded since 2003. We have grown from just selling pet food to appeal, toys and so much more!

We believe in providing our customers with the best of the best and is always looking for ways to improve. We work closely with the university and their research programs to find new ways to build our toys selection and the nutrition department to serve pets the healthiest foods. The majority of our staff members are students that are officiated with the school that specializes in animal care.

We strongly believe in bringing happiness and joy to the pets that lights up our world.
