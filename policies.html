Delivery

We believe in providing not only quality goods, but obtainable goods. We deliver products right to your door step once it's been paid for. You get to pick the time and day that's convenient for you! This only Apply during regular business hours, Monday-Friday. Unfortunately, we Only deliver within Durham, NH. Residents outside of Durham will need To pick up their products in person.
Return
Products are accepted as return if it's unused, new, and bought back within 30 days of purchase. We will only refund if you have the receipt. Purchases will not be accepted without a receipt. There will be no questions asked!
